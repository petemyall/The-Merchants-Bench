Every day behind the counter in a Builder's Merchants you lose count of how often you get challenged on the price you are quoting How Much, Are You Sure?, It's Cheaper Down the Road, Don't I Get My Trade Discount,it is constant and it all means the same thing, “Sell it to me as cheap as you can because I want to earn more from my current job than I had hoped for. Even though the builder in question drives a big flash car and regularly goes on expensive holidays meanwhile my salary stays down because the profit margin isn't where it should be.
