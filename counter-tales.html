Everyday Haggling

Every day behind the counter in a Builder's Merchants, you lose count of how often you're challenged on price:

  • “How much?!”
  • “Are you sure?”
  • “It’s cheaper down the road.”
  • “Don’t I get my trade discount?”

It's constant — and it all boils down to the same thing: “Sell it to me as cheap as you can so I can make a few quid more than I planned to.”

This from a bloke pulling up in a brand-new van, off on holiday next week, while I’m still working out if I can afford a steak bake at lunchtime.

When someone asked, “What’s your best price?” I’d give them a figure that made their eyebrows disappear into their hard hat. After the inevitable coughing fit, I’d grin and explain: “Well, that’s my best price — best for me, not for you.”
